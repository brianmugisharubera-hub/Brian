About Eco-Stream Logistics

Leading the charge toward sustainable delivery

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Eco-Stream Logistics began with a simple mission: prove that a delivery company could be both profitable and sustainable. What started as a small experiment with three electric vehicles has grown into a trusted partner for 500+ local businesses.

We recognized early that climate change wasn't just an environmental issue—it was a business imperative. Our founders invested in electric vehicle technology before it was mainstream, betting that businesses and consumers would eventually demand more sustainable options.

Today, we operate the largest all-electric delivery fleet in the region. We've prevented over 2,500 tons of CO₂ emissions from entering the atmosphere and have completed more than 50,000 sustainable deliveries.

Our Mission & Values

Sustainability First

We put environmental responsibility at the center of every decision. Our zero-emission fleet and eco-friendly practices prove that green business is good business.

Community Focused

We're embedded in the communities we serve. We support local businesses, employ local workers, and invest in local sustainability initiatives.

Excellence

We don't compromise on quality. Our commitment to timely, reliable delivery is as strong as our commitment to the planet.

Transparency

We believe in honest communication. What we promise, we deliver—both packages and environmental impact reports.
