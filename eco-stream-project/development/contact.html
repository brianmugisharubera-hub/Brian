Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you pick up my delivery?

We typically pick up orders within 2-4 hours of booking. Express services are available for urgent needs, and scheduled pickup can be arranged for regular customers.

Do you offer tracking?

Yes! All deliveries include real-time GPS tracking. You'll receive a tracking link via email and can monitor your shipment every step of the way.

What areas do you serve?

We operate within a 50-mile radius of our headquarters. For deliveries outside this area, please contact us to discuss custom solutions.

Are your vehicles insured?

Yes, all our vehicles are fully insured. We include standard coverage for packages up to $500 per item. Higher value items may require additional insurance.

Can you handle oversized items?

We can handle items up to 500 lbs. For oversized or specialty items, please contact us for a custom quote and availability.

What packaging do you recommend?

We recommend sturdy, well-sealed boxes. However, many customers use our eco-friendly packaging options. Ask about our sustainable packaging programs.
